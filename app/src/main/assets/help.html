Help:

My Spending application is a simple and easy-to-use app that helps you track your spending. With My Spendings app, you can:

  • Record all your spendings
  • Add, edit, delete your day to day expenses
  • View total expenses as daily, monthly or yearly
  • View the expenses in form of graph chart
  • Export your entries to a CSV file

How to use My Spending application

  1. Add Record

    To add a new spending record, tap the floating button or select "Add New" from the navigation drawer. Enter the details of the expense, such as the date, amount, category, and description. Tap "Save" to add the record.

  2. Delete Record

    To delete a spending record, swipe left on the record in the "All Spending" tab.

  3. Edit Record

    To edit a spending record, tap on the record in the "All Spending" tab. Make the changes you want and tap "Save".

  4. Report

    To view your spendings in a graph chart, select "Report" from the navigation drawer. You can choose to view your spendings by day, month, or year.

  5. Export

    To export your spending data to a CSV file, select "Export" from the navigation drawer. The CSV file will be saved to your device's downloads folder.
