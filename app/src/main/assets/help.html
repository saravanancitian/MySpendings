Help:

My Spending application help to record all your spendings through which you can keep track of your expanses.

  1. Add Record

    To Record your spending either press the floating button or use "Add New" in navigation drawer, which takes you to the Edit/New window, where you have to fill in the details and save the record. The record will appear in the all transactions.

  2. Delete Record

    You can delete a spending record in "All Spending" tab in main window, by swiping left the item record.

  3. Edit Record

    upon clicking the spending record in "All Spending" tab in main window, A pop window with record information will appear. "Edit" button in the pop will take you to the Edit/New window, where you can edit and save the record.

  4. Report

    Your spendings can be visualized in form of chart in report window. You can view report window by selecting "report" item in navigation drawer.
